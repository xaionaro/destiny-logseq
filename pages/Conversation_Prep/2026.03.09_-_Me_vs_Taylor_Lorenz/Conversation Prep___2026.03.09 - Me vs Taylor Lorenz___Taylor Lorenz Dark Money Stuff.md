- # Tax Stuff
  - 501(c)(4)
    - Social Welfare Organization
    - Can engage in a limited amount of political activity, so long as it's not the primary purpose of the organization.
    - Can fundraise an unlimited amount of money per donor.
    - Must disclose contributions on a 990 Schedule B, but can redact the names/addresses of the contributors. ([2025 Instructions for Form 990 Return of Organization Exempt From Income Tax](https://www.irs.gov/pub/irs-pdf/i990.pdf))
